Rule Engine Notes

  • You must have at least 2 battalions in the attacking territory to attack.
  • The attacker always rolls the maximum legal number of dice for the battalions available.
  • The defender always rolls the maximum legal number of dice for the battalions present.
  • Leader and stronghold bonuses stack on the defender’s highest die; a defender leader plus stronghold gives +2.
  • After a successful invasion, surviving attacking battalions from the final battle must move in first.

Combat Rules Modeled

  • Attacker compares highest die to defender highest die, then next-highest against next-highest.
  • Only as many comparisons as the smaller dice pool.
  • In ties, the defender wins.
  • Bonuses modify only the side’s single highest die for that battle.
  • Attack ends if the attacker stops, cannot continue, or loses all battalions except one in the origin territory.

Capture Resolution

  • When defenders are eliminated, the territory is conquered immediately.
  • All surviving attackers from the last battle move in automatically.
  • Optional extra battalions may also move in, but at least one battalion must remain behind in the origin territory.
  • This app focuses on battle resolution, not region scoring, card draws, fellowship movement, or mission/adventure card play.
